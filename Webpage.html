01 Flipkart

Flipkart is a popular e-commerce platform in India that offers a wide range of products from various categories,such as smartphones, laptops, appliances, furniture, books, beauty, toys and more.It was initially focused on online book sales before expanding into other product categories. Flipkart is headquartered in Bangalore, India and incorporated in Singapore as a private limited company .
02 Instagram

Instagram is a popular photo and video sharing social networking service owned by Meta Platforms . It allows users to upload media that can be edited with filters, be organized by hashtags, and be associated with a location via geographical tagging . Posts can be shared publicly or with preapproved followers . Instagram also offers a feature called Reels which allows users to create, share, and watch short, entertaining videos on Instagram . Users can also check out Stories and live videos from their favorite people . Instagram has a messaging feature called Direct which allows users to send messages, photos, and videos to a friend or select group of people . Instagram also provides a feature called Shopping which allows users to browse the latest trends from their favorite brands and creators .

05 LinkedIn

LinkedIn is a social networking platform that allows users to connect with other professionals, build their professional network, and find job opportunities . It is a great platform for job seekers, recruiters, and businesses to connect and share information. LinkedIn has a user-friendly interface that allows users to create a profile, upload their resume, and connect with other professionals in their field. Users can also join groups, follow companies, and participate in discussions to stay up-to-date with the latest industry news and trends. LinkedIn is available as a mobile app on both Android and iOS platforms.
